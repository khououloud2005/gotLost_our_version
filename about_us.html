What Do We Provide?

  1. A trustworthy community.
  2. More honest people in our world.
  3. Less stress in college life.
  4. Connections with new people.
  5. Better communication.
  6. Transparency and accountability.

We help match lost items with their owners.

Did This Happen To You?

We’ve all lost something valuable at college. We know how it feels.

So we created a system that allows you to recover your lost items.

You will help each other!

How Does It Work?

On the homepage, click on "Lost" or "Found".

Make sure to register first: Register here.

Lost or Found Something?

Click "FOUND" to report something you've found.

Click "LOST" to report something you've lost.

What Data Do We Collect?

  1. Email address.
  2. Details of lost items.
  3. Details of found items.

How Do We Connect People?

  1. Lost item reports receive match notifications for 14 days.
  2. A Q&A ensures the right owner gets the item.
  3. Unclaimed items are safely stored.
